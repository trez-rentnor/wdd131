About Me

My name is Randy Jones. I am from San Diego, California. I have been a software developer for nearly twenty years. I have worked on embedded code for everything from set top boxes to prosthetic legs.

I'm also working on getting a startup running. It's called Stavely, and is a mobile app to help musicians notate and share music. Check out my web page!

San Diego, California

San Diego is in the southwest of California, right across the border from Tijuana, Mexico. The Pacific Ocean borders the city on the west, providing abundant beaches. The military has a large presence, with major training facilities for both the Navy and Marines. San Diego was the home of the Navy Top Gun pilot training that the movie was based on. In fact, lot of the movie was filmed in San Diego!
