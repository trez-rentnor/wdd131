TheaterPlan Site Plan

1. Site Name

TheaterPlan describes this tool, which makes scheduling play rehearslas easier by laying them out in a clear way and keeping everybody up to date.

2. Purpose

Scheduling rehearsals is one of the most grueling parts of running a production. Laying out a schedule, planning who needs to attend, and making sure everybody is kept up to date is a challenge for directors at every level from beginner youth theater through Broadway productions. TheatherPlan makes that easier.

3. Scenarios

  • The director plans rehearsals, including which actors are participating
  • Actors can see their rehearsal schedules
  • The director can see a list of all reheasals
